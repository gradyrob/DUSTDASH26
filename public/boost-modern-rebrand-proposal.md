# Boost Exterior Cleaning Website Upgrade Proposal (Concept)

## What This Upgrade Solves
- Modern visual identity and stronger first impression.
- Cleaner mobile experience with faster access to quote and call actions.
- Better conversion flow with clearer CTA hierarchy and reduced friction.
- Improved readability and trust presentation (services, process, results, testimonials).

## Included In This Concept
- New one-page redesign:
  - `public/boost-modern-rebrand.html`
- Mobile-first navigation and sticky mobile actions.
- Modern quote form UX mapped to existing contact workflow.
- Service and process storytelling based on current site context:
  - Soft washing
  - Pressure cleaning
  - Driveway cleaning and sealing
  - Stone tile and paver care
  - Residential, commercial, and school cleaning
  - SE QLD coverage (Brisbane, Logan, Gold Coast, Buderim)

## Conversion Improvements
- Hero section now communicates value proposition immediately.
- Phone and quote actions are always visible on both desktop and mobile.
- Service cards and process sections reduce buyer uncertainty.
- Contact section combines trust + form + direct call options.

## Suggested Commercial Positioning
- "Not a cosmetic refresh; this is a lead-generation upgrade."
- "Built to convert mobile traffic first, where most quote requests happen."
- "Keeps your existing business message, but presents it at modern premium standard."

## Suggested Upgrade Package To Sell
- Design + build of new homepage.
- Conversion-focused copy polish across key sections.
- Technical handover for live deployment on existing domain.
- Optional phase 2: service pages, franchise page redesign, SEO schema/content uplift.
