Soft Wash System

The Boost Exterior Cleaning Method

Soft washing uses low pressure and specialised solutions to kill and remove mould, algae and organic staining at the source, not just the surface layer.

1

Inspect & Assess

We identify material types, staining severity and select the right pressure and chemistry mix for each area.

2

Pre-Treat Surface

Specialist biodegradable solutions are applied to break down mould, algae and grime before rinsing.

3

Controlled Wash

Low-pressure soft wash or high-pressure rotary cleaning is used based on the surface and contamination level.

4

Protect & Finish

Optional sealing and final detailing help keep surfaces cleaner for longer and improve long-term results.
