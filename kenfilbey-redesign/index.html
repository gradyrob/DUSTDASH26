Welcome

Welcome to Ken Filbey Financial Services

We will be there through life's ups and downs to help you make the best possible financial decisions - to protect your current wealth and help you to create a better financial future.

Our aim is to develop the best financial solutions to match your particular circumstances:

  • We take the time to understand your exact financial and lifestyle objectives.
  • We develop relevant strategies to help you meet these objectives and make sure you understand, and are comfortable with, the plan.
  • We make sure the agreed strategies are implemented.
  • We keep you updated regularly on the progress towards your objectives.

All this is done on a personal basis - in our view, nothing replaces face-to-face advice.

Profile

Local, experienced, and focused on your peace of mind

Established in 1991, Ken Filbey Financial Services is a locally owned and operated business and we provide an extensive range of Risk Insurance products and Financial Planning to suit the personal, financial and business requirements of our clientele.

We offer years of combined experience, knowledge and trust, totally committed to your insurance and financial needs and most importantly your peace of mind.

We have offices in Ballarat, Victoria and Mooloolaba, Queensland covering all areas in South East Queensland, offering Risk Insurance, Superannuation and Financial Planning products and services.

Ken Filbey Financial Services prides itself in offering personalised, friendly service.
